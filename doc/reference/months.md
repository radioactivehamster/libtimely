## Julian and Gregorian calendars
The Gregorian calendar, like the Julian calendar before it, has twelve months:

| Month      | Days                      |
| -----------|---------------------------|
| January    | 31 days                   |
| February   | 28 days, 29 in leap years |
| March      | 31 days                   |
| April      | 30 days                   |
| May        | 31 days                   |
| June       | 30 days                   |
| July       | 31 days                   |
| August     | 31 days                   |
| September  | 30 days                   |
| October    | 31 days                   |
| November   | 30 days                   |
| December   | 31 days                   |
